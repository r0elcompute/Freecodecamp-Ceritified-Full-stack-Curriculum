A boy develops robotic prostethic arm

Kenyan boy shocks the world with tech innovation!

-Rodgers Fanuel Mwangi

A kenyan boy named Francis Githau hit the newsline when he came up with his very own robotic prosthetic arm.
He is a 14 year old grade six student at the Kidzone Tango school in a small neighbourhood in Mombasa, Nyali.
People were amazed by his innovation and even wondered how a young child could have
the skillset to come up with such a complex piece of device that not everyone is capable of building.
This drew closer the attention of tech companies across Kenya and Africa at large, with CEO s asking for the whereabout of the young man.

In a recent headline, Francis opened up about receiving a scholarship and contract to work with ipoa Kenya to make sure his dream
becomes a reality for many disabled people across Africa and even the world at large! Pretty amazing right?
